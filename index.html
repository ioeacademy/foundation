Quantum · Classical · Visual

From a single bit to entanglement.

A collection of interactive simulators that visualize the leap from classical computing to quantum mechanics — each running entirely in your browser.

01 · Classical

Classical Bit

Switch, voltmeter, state-space, digital logic gates, and the linear-combination math view — four perspectives on a single classical bit.

Physical Logic Gates Math
Open simulator
02 · Atomic

Hydrogen Spectroscopy

3D explorer of the Hydrogen atom across Bohr, de Broglie, and Schrödinger models. Tune photon energy, find resonances on Lyman & Balmer series.

3D / Three.js Photon Absorption Orbitals
Open simulator
03 · Quantum

Qubit

Single-qubit playground — superposition, the Bloch sphere, and gate operations. The quantum analogue of the classical bit.

Bloch Sphere Superposition Gates
Open simulator
04 · Quantum

Entanglement

Two-qubit circuit composer. Drag H, X, Z, CNOT gates onto the wires and watch Bell states emerge — with full matrix and Dirac derivations.

Bell States Tensor Product CNOT
Open simulator

Built with vanilla HTML, Tailwind, Three.js, and MathJax. No backend — everything runs locally in the browser.
